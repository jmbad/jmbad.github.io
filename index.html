About

We are an architecture and design studio based in Paris. We make architecture, but also furniture, exhibitions, objects, temporary things, speculative things. We try not to do the same thing twice. Our work often sits somewhere between disciplines, and our process leans heavily on making: drawing, building, testing, failing, trying again.

Clients

We work with cultural institutions, municipalities, private clients, and the occasional friend. Recent projects include works for Villa Noailles, Chanel, Concentrico Festival, Beta Architecture Biennial.

Press

Our work has been featured in the Architectural Review, Archdaily, Architectural Digest France and Spain, IDEAT, Télérama.

Distinctions
